My Favorite Polynomial

Explanation

I chose this polynomial because its roots are the first five terms of the Fibonacci sequence. This sequence starts with 1 and 1, and each term afterwards is the sum of the previous two terms. This sequence is not just mathematical, though. These numbers tend to show up in nature very often, like in the number of petals on flowers. It perfectly illustrates my favorite aspect of evolution, the weird mathematical numbers and constants that tend to show up everywhere you look in nature. Another added benefit was that since this polynomial's roots are all small integers, it was very easy to test out my program, since most of the second column results should have been zero, and would have been far off the mark had there been a typo in my code.
