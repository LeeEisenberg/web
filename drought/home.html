Conserve H2O


Although droughts are a natural phenomenon, they are very dangerous and can cause many problems for people and the environment.

Droughts can be caused by many things, but the main cause is a lack of precipitation. This can be caused by climate change, which is caused by humans.

Our goal with this website is to raise awareness about droughts and how they can be prevented. We hope that you will learn something new here.
